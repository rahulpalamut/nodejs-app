Why?

We feel that finding Bacon is major hassle for everyone. So we decided to make a bacon locator that will help the bacon depraved fulfill their bacon desires. In exchange we ask you to think about those of us who cannot afford bacon. We hope that by fulfilling your bacon needs, you will make a donation or help out a homeless individual.

How?

We scrape UCSC's dining website using kimonolabs, store the data in Mongo DB...
